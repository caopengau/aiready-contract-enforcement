# Storyboard: Day 0 - Why OpenClaw?

## Visual Identity

- **Theme**: "The Goldfish Effect" vs. "Continuous Consciousness."
- **Colors**: Deep industrial blues, electric amber pulses (for the Living Circuit).
- **Vibe**: Tech-noir but optimistic; the feeling of uncovering a hidden truth.
