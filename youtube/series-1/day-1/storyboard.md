# Storyboard: Day 1 - The Brain (LLM Fundamentals as Logic)

## Visual Identity

- **Themes**: Logic Bricks, Probability Waves, The "Lego" Blueprint.
- **Vibe**: Analytical, clean, instructional.
- **Primary Visual**: Holographic blocks that light up as they "connect."

---

## Production Scenes

### Scene 1: Beyond the Magic (0:00 - 1:45)

- **Visual**:
  - Montage of AI "Hype": Sparkles, floating brains, magical glows.
  - Hard Cut: A black terminal screen with white text scrolling: `Predicting Next Token...`
  - Split screen: "Magic" (Cloudy, vague) vs. "Engineering" (Sharp, defined lines).
- **Reference Asset**: <ASSET: youtube/assets/branding/s1_branding_hero.png>

### Scene 2: The Next Token Game (1:45 - 4:00)

- **Visual**:
  - Animation of a slot machine or ticker-tape showing words spinning and landing on the most probable choice.
  - Probability bar charts appearing over code snippets.
- **Reference Asset**: <ASSET: youtube/assets/branding/s1_d1_llm_logic_gates.png>

### Scene 3: The Smart Brick Metaphor (4:00 - 6:30)

- **Visual**:
  - A real-world Lego block on a table.
  - A holographic overlay turns it into a "Smart Brick" with data flowing into it.
  - Montage: Bricks stacking to form a complex "Bridge" (The Agent).
- **Reference Asset**: <ASSET: youtube/assets/branding/s1_d1_llm_logic_gates.png> (The "Logic Bricks" view).

### Scene 4: Hallucination & Context (6:30 - 8:30)

- **Visual**:
  - A worker (animated) trying to build a bridge but the manual is in pieces (Fragmentation).
  - The worker starts guessing, and the bridge collapses.
  - Fix: The worker gets a tablet with a clear blueprint (AIReady code), and the bridge stabilizes.
- **Metaphor**: "The Goldfish Effect" from Day 0, briefly revisited.

### Scene 5: Wrapping Up (8:30 - 10:00)

- **Visual**:
  - The "Smart Brick" from Scene 3 is shown connecting to a "Tool" gear and a "Memory" chip (Preview of Day 2 & 3).
  - Fade to SuperPeng.
- **Reference Asset**: <ASSET: youtube/assets/branding/s1_branding_hero.png>
